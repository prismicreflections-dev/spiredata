What AI & Advanced Analytics Can Do?

Advanced Analytics

In your digital enterprise, how do you drive innovative business decision-making? If you’re still using traditional ways, it’s time for a reboot. Our sophisticated tools can conduct data mining and big data analytics and reporting to detect key trends, patterns, and performance.

Neural Network & Deep Learning

Deep learning uses artificial neural networks and algorithms inspired by the human brain to learn from a massive amount of data. As humans learn from experience, the process performs a task repeatedly, improving it each time to create a learning loop.

Computer Vision

Your data may comprise countless digital images, but how can you sift through all? Computer vision uses specialized methods and general learning to replicate the capacities of human vision. We put the multi-disciplinary field of computer vision to work across applications.

Predictive Analytics

Data, statistical algorithms and ML techniques can identify future outcomes based on historical data. Predictive analytics uses what has happened to assess what will happen in the future and uncover new opportunities. Detect fraud, optimize marketing campaigns and more.

Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing (NLP) uses algorithms to read text, hear and interpret speech, measure sentiment, and determine critical parts. NLP helps resolve ambiguity in language by structuring an unstructured data source and adding numeric structure to data for speech recognition, text analytics, and more.

AI-enabled Mobile Apps & Software

Did you know that AI can personalize your app? Through insight derivation, task automation and IoT integration, and pattern recognition, AI powers apps and software to show related content and ads. This improves engagement and productivity by meeting user expectations consistently.
